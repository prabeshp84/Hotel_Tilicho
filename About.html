The Beginning

Prabesh Poudel was the one who initially focused on technology and innovation. Instead of jumping straight into the hospitality industry, he spent several years developing cutting-edge systems and software for businesses. After gaining substantial experience and a successful track record in tech, he returned to the resort venture with a fresh perspective. His expertise in technology played a crucial role in transforming Hotel Tilicho into a luxurious, world-renowned resort by integrating advanced management systems and creating a top-notch guest experience.
